About Hannah Schroeder

Interests & Aspirations

Hannah Schroeder is a professional with a passion for data-driven decision-making, strategy, and product management, specifically in healthcare and education sectors. Her expertise lies in curriculum management, content development, and strategic planning, with a focus on leveraging analytics to inform product development and optimize educational outcomes.

In 2018, Hannah obtained a Master of Science in Nursing Education from Capella University. In pursuit of professional advancement, she is currently enrolled at the Quantic School of Business and Technology, where she was accepted into the MSBA, Master of Science in Business Analytics, program. She hopes to learn from, and collaborate with, international experts while gaining the knowledge, skills, and experience necessary to advance her career through this acclaimed MSBA program.

She is excited to apply sophisticated data analysis and visualization techniques during opportunities to evaluate content efficacy and performance, conduct and explore market research, and execute strategic decision-making. With a track record of academic excellence and continuous learning, Hannah excels in managing complex projects, fostering cross-functional collaboration, and integrating innovative technologies.

Hannah's ultimate career goal is to combine her analytical thinking and expertise to improve outcomes and drive innovation in fields related to healthcare and social justice initiatives.
